MEET MELODY OF HOPE

Melody of Hope is an electic team of vibrant youthful professionals inspired to change the world starting with North Texas. We are scattered across DFW, yet unite under a singular vision: to see music bring hope to our local community. What you see on the left is a formidable force. The short period of time in which this team has assembled is indicative of the momentum at which Melody of Hope has plowed forward. The volume of financial donors, organization partners, and event sponsors MoH has acquired is telling of the charisma and unity with which we operate. Needless to say, meetings are fun, exciting and full of laughter. If you don't know us, we hope to meet you soon!
